And our group thinks that what Professor Mason Cooley said in the article is true because in most places in the Philippines are full of garbage especially after big events/occasions.

We came up with the idea of making a mini seminar, so that the poeple will know the bad and good effects of having overflowing trashes. Also, they can get knowledge about our topic and probably they will lessen the wastes that can clog the trashes and sewers. We basically know that some of us were concern about our environment but others are not. With that, we will attract them through our seminar. Through this, they will realize that they are not following the rules of segregating and cleanings the environment. Though they it doesn't mean that they are not literally following the rules, but somehow. They are some lazy people.
